ABOUT THE EVENT

A Celebration of Faith & Flavor

Join us for a weekend of culinary delights from around the world! The St. Anthony the Great Orthodox Church International Food Festival brings together our diverse community to share traditional recipes, cultural heritage, and the joy of fellowship.

From authentic Mediterranean dishes to international specialties, experience the tastes that have been passed down through generations. All proceeds support our church ministries and community outreach programs.

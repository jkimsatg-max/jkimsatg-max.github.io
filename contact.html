Frequently Asked Questions

Is parking free?

Yes! Free parking is available on-site at the church and at the neighboring park. Shuttle service provided from remote lots.

Can I bring my dog?

Service animals are welcome. Please leave pets at home due to food service areas and large crowds.

What payment methods are accepted?

Admission can be paid by cash or card. Food vendors use ticket system—ATMs available on-site.

Are there vegetarian options?

Absolutely! Many Orthodox fasting dishes are naturally vegan. Look for (V) markers on menu items.

Is the festival rain or shine?

Yes! Most vendors are under tents, and the church hall provides indoor space for some activities.

Can I volunteer for just one day?

Yes! We welcome volunteers for single shifts or multiple days. Visit the volunteer tent on arrival.
